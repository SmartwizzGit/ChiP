Features ChiP provides a wide array of investment banking and securities services in India with offices in Mumbai and New Delhi. This includes a full range of equity underwriting and financial advisory solutions to Indian corporates as well as to MNCs looking to build their presence in the Indian market.
Corporate Finance The Corporate Finance team provides a full range of advisory services including mergers and acquisitions, disposals, strategic investments, capital restructurings and spin-offs and advisory services on fixed income products. The team has professionals with substantial investment banking experience in India. Key features of our execution capabilities include: complete support for cross-border transactions, capability in developing innovative structures and the ability to provide new products in India's changing environment.
Structured Finance The Structured Finance team in India works with corporates to provide advice on financing across the entire capital structure and across asset classes. It works closely with the various coverage teams to originate and execute all structured finance transactions for the bank's clients as part of the Global Debt Capital Markets Group.
Equity Capital Markets The Equity Capital Markets Group is responsible for activities in the primary equity and equity-linked markets in India. It works closely with the Corporate Finance team for origination of primary business from Indian corporates and financial institutions. Additionally, it manages the structuring, marketing, syndication and distribution of equity and equity-related offerings. In this role, it also draws on the resources of our strong Indian institutional equities department.
